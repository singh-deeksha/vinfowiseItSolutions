## Typography

Theme: `NeoTech Enterprise`

Headings: `Inter`

Body/UI: `Poppins`

Fallback: `Arial, sans-serif`

## Logo Direction

The mark keeps the previous winged `V` and star idea, but redraws it as an original modern technology emblem with cleaner geometry, gradients, and web-ready SVG output.

## Usage Notes

Use the emblem for favicons, app icons, and compact header spaces. Use the full square logo when the text has enough room. Use the horizontal logo for documents, proposals, cover pages, and social banners. Use the social profile SVG for LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, and WhatsApp display images.
